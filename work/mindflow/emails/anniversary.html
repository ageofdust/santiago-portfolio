One year in,
and I've been thinking about you.

Hi friend,

I'm Gustavo, founder of MindFlow. We just hit our one-year mark, and I've been reaching out to people who joined us early on.

I know it's been a while since you've opened the app. No worries — MindFlow isn't built for streaks. It's built for when something comes up: a dream, a thought, a feeling you want to sit with.

For our anniversary, we asked the people who've been using MindFlow since the beginning a single question: if MindFlow disappeared tomorrow, what would you lose?

Their answers stayed with me.

None of them described losing a tool. They described losing a place.

So we've kept building that place, for the moments that bring you back.
