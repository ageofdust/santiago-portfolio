Stage 02 · Activation

This only works if you come back to it

First 7 days after signup. Move from first interaction to early continuity. 4–6 emails total, push for app users.

Direct and actionable. Acknowledge actions, not inner content.

The Mindflow habit loop
Reflect → Understand → Reframe → Reset → Rest

Introduced in Activation. Reinforced through every stage that follows.

01 Reflect Capture what's present, without filtering
→
02 Understand Return to what was captured. See it with distance.
→
03 Reframe Place it in a broader context. Notice connection.
→
04 Reset Create space between recognition and reaction
→
05 Rest Let the process continue without forcing it
↻ The loop continues. Clarity builds through repetition, not pressure.
